Honey Garlic Roasted Carrots

Today we are going to learn how to make Ms. Pookies famous and fabulous side.

Ingredients

  • 1lb of carrots
  • 6tsp of honey
  • 1/2 stick of butter
  • 3 cloves of Garlic
  • 2tsp of salt
  • 2tsp of pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees, and melt the 1/2 stick of butter.

  2. While the oven heats, begin cutting the carrots into small bite size chips. Then grab the garlic and roughly chop them to be used in our seasoning.

  3. After the butter has melted place it into a bowl. Add in the fresh garlic, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined and add in the honey. After couple minutes of mixing add in the cut carrots and toss until each carrot is evenly coated. Testter.

  4. Place carrots onto a baking pan ensuring that they are evenly distributed. Then Place the sheet into the oven for 20 minutes. If extra roasting is required broil the carrots for an additional 5 minutes.

  5. The final most important step is to enjoy your carrots with a loved one.
